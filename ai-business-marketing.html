AI for Business and Marketing

Smarter Marketing with AI

Mode: Online / Offline

Duration: Timing will be fixed as per your convenience

Course Syllabus

Module 1: Basics & Fundamentals

  • Fundamentals of Marketing, Branding, and Customer Segmentation
  • Introduction to Digital Marketing platforms
  • Types of Online Businesses and Objectives
  • Overview of AI applications in Marketing: Personalization, Automation, Analytics, Etc..

Module 2: AI Tools & Techniques

  • AI Tools for Market Analysis, Campaign Creation (ChatGPT, Jasper, Midjourney, Etc..)
  • Predictive Analytics and Customer Experience Personalization
  • Latest AI Techniques & Applications
  • Predictive Analytics for Customer Segmentation & Lifetime Value (Salesforce Einstein, Google Vertex AI)

Module 3: AI for Social Media, Content & Ads

  • Social Media AI (automation & insights using Meta AI, Google AI tools)
  • AI-driven Content Marketing and Lead Generation
  • AI-powered Social Listening & Sentiment Analysis (Brand24, Gumloop)
  • Ad Creation and Programmatic Ad Buying: Smart bidding, responsive ad design (Google Ads AI, Arcads, Meta Advantage+)
  • Generative AI for Content Creation: Blogs, newsletters, video synthesis, images (ChatGPT, Jasper, Midjourney, Runway ML)
  • AI-Powered Email Marketing Automation: Dynamic segmentation, triggered emails, subject line optimization (Persado, Customer.io, SendGrid AI)

Module 4: Analytics, Personalization & Reporting

  • Automated Report Generation and Campaign Dashboards (Tableau AI, Power BI with Copilot, Claude Artifacts)
  • Real-time Personalization Engines: Product recommendations (Amazon, Netflix, Shopify AI, Insider GMP)

Module 5: Advanced AI Applications in Marketing

  • Chatbots & Conversational AI (Dialogflow, Drift, Intercom AI)
  • NLP for Brand Monitoring & Market Research: Text mining reviews, feedback, competitor analysis
  • Predictive Sales Forecasting: Deep learning on sales data, trends & inventory management

Module 6: Ethics, Bias & Compliance

  • Ethical, Bias, and Compliance Modules: Data privacy, responsible use, bias detection tools
  • Case study analysis (GDPR, CCPA focus)

Hands-On & Projects

  • Build an AI-powered marketing dashboard (social media sentiment, competitor tracking, campaign metrics)
  • Design an AI-powered multi-channel marketing campaign
  • Real-world case studies: Brand personalization at Amazon/Netflix/Shopify
  • Implement generative AI for video, blog, and ad creation

Capstone Project (Industry Standard)

  • Complete AI-driven marketing campaign: Strategy, execution, optimization, reporting, compliance
  • Present insights, dashboards, and recommendations using advanced AI tools
  • Critical evaluation: ROI analysis, ethical risks/solutions in AI marketing
