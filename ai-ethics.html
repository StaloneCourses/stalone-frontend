AI Ethics and Complaince for Future Leaders

Lead with integrity in the age of AI — master ethics, compliance, and governance for a responsible future

Mode: Online / Offline

Duration: Timing will be fixed as per your convenience

Course Syllabus

Module 1 :

  • Core Concepts: AI Ethics, Bias, Fairness, Transparency
  • Introduction to Responsible AI frameworks and standards (GDPR, CCPA)

Module 2 :

  • Addressing Bias in Data and Algorithms
  • Case Studies: AI Failures, Compliance Breaches, Industry best practices
  • Stakeholder Analysis & Policy Development

Module 3 :

  • In-depth study of global AI regulations: EU AI Act, US federal standards, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF, Take It Down Act (for deepfakes)
  • Synthesizing compliance checklists for high-risk AI applications (finance, healthcare, HR)
  • AI governance best practices—roles (Chief AI Ethics Officer), policy development, stakeholder engagement, and documentation
  • Legal ramifications of AI misuse: real-world case studies, fines, and legal actions

Module 4 :

  • Comprehensive AI auditing standards: data lineage, model behavior, deployment documentation, decision and explanation logs
  • Quantitative risk models and impact assessments for AI—measurement techniques and tools
  • Continuous monitoring: real-time risk evaluation, bias scoring, and model drift detection in production

Module 5 :

  • Technical bias mitigation: adversarial debiasing, reweighing, and counterfactual fairness techniques
  • Algorithmic transparency: stakeholder involvement, end-to-end audit trails, and outcome validation
  • Fairness-aware design in ML pipelines, with hands-on labs using real-world biased datasets

Module 6 :

  • Industry-leading model explainability tools: LIME, SHAP, AI Explainability 360, saliency maps
  • Metrics for evaluating explanations: fidelity, sensitivity, complexity, user trust
  • Emerging techniques: explainable RL, GNN interpretability, local/global explanations
  • Human-centered explanations and compliance with major standards

Module 7 :

  • Differential privacy, federated learning, and privacy-by-design architectures
  • Red-teaming AI systems for robustness and security
  • AI-specific data handling: synthetic data, consent management, edge-case privacy attacks

Module 8 :

  • Designing AI for augmented decision-making
  • Trust calibration, over-reliance prevention, job impact assessments
  • Societal case studies: AI deployment in public sector/critical infrastructure

Module 9 :

  • Full-stack AI audit for bias, transparency, compliance (real model)
  • Build explainability dashboards for black-box models
  • Scenario-based risk assessment: simulate compliance breach & mitigation

Module 10 - Capstone Project :

  • Design & deploy an AI system for HR/Finance/Healthcare meeting all global standards
  • End-to-end: bias-mitigation, audit logs, explainability API, compliance package
  • Present findings to a simulated regulator panel
