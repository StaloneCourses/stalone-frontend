AI Content Creation

From Ideas to Impact: Build Next-Gen Content with AI

Mode: Online / Offline

Duration: Timing will be fixed as per your convenience

Course Syllabus

Module 1: Introduction to Generative AI

  • Introduction to Generative AI: Text, Images, Video and Audio
  • AI Tools for Writers, Marketers, Designers, Etc.
  • Copyright, Fair Use and Responsible AI Creativity

Module 2: Intermediate - Content Automation

  • Automated Content Creation Workflows (social media posts, blogs, ads)
  • Video Editing and Creation with AI (e.g., Synthesia, Runway ML)
  • Interactive and Multimodal Content (combining text, images, etc.)
  • Data-driven content personalization

Module 3: Advanced Fundamentals & Enterprise Tools

  • Multimodal generative AI (text, image, video, audio with GPT-4, Sora, Midjourney, Runway ML, GenApe, etc.)
  • Latest transformer and diffusion models for each content type (LLaMA, SDXL, Gemini Pro/Vision)
  • Content creation pipelines in business: Workflow automation using Jasper, Writesonic, Copy.ai, SurferSEO, Zapier, etc.

Module 4: Prompt Engineering & Fine-Tuning

  • Advanced prompt strategies for generative platforms (persona, style, format, context stacking)
  • Data-driven fine-tuning and custom model deployment (OpenAI API, HuggingFace tools, Google AI Studio)
  • Testing and optimizing for consistency, brand voice, and audience engagement

Module 5: Video & Image Creation Techniques

  • Text-to-video (Sora, Runway ML, GenApe, Bing Image Creator)
  • Image-to-video workflows (Canva AI, Wan AI, Vidful, Magi) with animation, filters, and special effects
  • Synthetic avatars, lip-sync, and multilingual generation (Synthesia, D-ID, Canva AI Avatars)
  • Real-time creation and editing for social media, advertising, product demos using enterprise platforms

Module 6: Text Generation & Multimodal Content

  • Automated blog, ad copy, newsletters, long-form text with Jasper, ChatGPT, Copy.ai
  • SEO optimization and plagiarism detection for AI-written content (SurferSEO, Originality AI, Brandwell, ContentShake AI)
  • Integrating text, image, and video into coordinated campaigns (workflow hacks and automation)

Module 7: Personalization, Analytics & Performance

  • Predictive performance analysis and A/B testing on AI content (Ekamoira, Sprinklr, Coworker.ai, FullStory)
  • Audience behavior modeling and segmentation for hyper-personalized content at scale
  • Attribution tracking—measuring conversion, engagement, ROI

Module 8: Ethics, Compliance & Human Validation

  • Deepfake, copyright, and fair-use management in generative campaigns
  • AI hallucination, bias, and disinformation detection strategies
  • Workflow for human-in-the-loop QA and compliance (enterprise standards: GDPR, SOC2, CASA Tier 2)

Module 9: Hands-On & Projects

  • Live automation labs: Social media video generation, blog workflows, campaign creation with enterprise tools
  • Capstone: Develop and launch a complete multimodal campaign for a product or brand using multiple AI tools
  • Team-based real-world challenge: Optimize creative production for quality, speed, and impact
