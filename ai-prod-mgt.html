Mode: Online / Offline

Course Syllabus

Module 1: Introduction to AI Product Management

  • Overview of AI in product lifecycle
  • Key roles and stakeholders in AI product management
  • Case studies of successful AI products

Module 2: Fundamentals of AI & Machine Learning Concepts

  • AI terminology and types (Supervised, Unsupervised, Reinforcement)
  • Understanding AI model outputs and performance metrics

Module 3: Market Research & Problem Identification

  • Identifying business problems for AI solutions
  • Competitive analysis and opportunity sizing

Module 4: Product Strategy & Vision for AI Solutions

  • Defining AI product vision and roadmap
  • Value proposition and KPIs for AI products

Module 5: No-Code AI Tools for Product Deployment

  • Overview of no-code AI platforms (e.g., Google AutoML, Microsoft Power Platform)
  • Integration methods with existing business systems

Module 6: Collaborating with AI Engineers & Data Scientists

  • Translating business needs to technical teams
  • Agile product management for AI

Module 7: AI Product Launch, Marketing & Customer Feedback

  • Go-to-market strategies for AI products
  • Managing customer feedback and iterative improvements

Module 8: Ethics, Compliance & Responsible AI in Product Management

  • AI bias, privacy, and regulatory considerations
  • Building trust and transparency in AI products
